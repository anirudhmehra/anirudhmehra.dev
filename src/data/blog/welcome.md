Welcome to my website.

I am using this space as a home for writing about engineering, cloud infrastructure, web development, and the tools that make software teams more effective.

Over time, this blog will collect:

- practical notes from day-to-day development work
- longer essays on systems, reliability, and developer experience
- occasional project writeups and experiments

The goal is simple: publish useful ideas clearly and keep the site fast, readable, and easy to maintain.
